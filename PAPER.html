About

Paper suitable for recycling is called "scrap paper", often used to produce moulded pulp packaging.

About half of all recovered paper comes from converting losses (pre-consumer recycling), such as shavings and unsold periodicals; approximately one third comes from household or post-consumer waste.

Benefits of recycling :
75% savings in energy.
90% savings in raw materials used.
86% reduction in air pollution.
40% reduction in water use.
76% reduction in water pollution.
97% reduction in mining wastes.
